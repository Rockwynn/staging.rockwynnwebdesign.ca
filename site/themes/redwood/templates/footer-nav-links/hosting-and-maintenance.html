Website Hosting & Maintenance

The cost of hosting & maintaining your website is $600 per year, billed annually. Or $50.00 monthly.

I will provide you with minor updates, they should not exceed more than 50% of the content on any single site page. The maintenance plan includes 1 hour of work each month. If you request work that will take more than 1 hour in a given month, I’ll ask you to confirm your plans and bill you at $60.00 per hour. During this time, you agree that I will be the sole provider of maintenance services for your Website, and no other party will have access to or rights to change your Website. If a party other than myself makes changes to the Website, any errors that are created, that must be repaired will be charged for separately.

In the event of non-payment of your hosting fees, your hosting account will be suspended until payment is received. I am not required to store your website files on the servers if your payment is 30 days or more past due. Please notify me of your intention to cancel your hosting account prior to your files being deleted so I may provide you with a backup of your site for a $100 fee.
